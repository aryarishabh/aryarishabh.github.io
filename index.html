We at KLUVOR provide support services to companies enabling them to create a positive impact on their bottom line and at the same time improve quality of core activities, encourage innovation, and reduce turnaround time.

With Kluvor as your global partner, you will have employees working at Kluvor offices in India but they will function as your extended team. The employees outsourced with Kluvor will follow all your processes and best practices and use your enterprise tools & software just like your own employees in your home country.

Quality in our services is ensured through peer reviews, standardized operating procedures, customised control mechanisms, and automation. Our ability to work overnight because of the time zone difference ensures quick turnaround time for deliverables. The exact services which we provide to our clients vary with their internal workflows and applications/software used in their companies. These services can range from regular data entry operations like payroll and invoice processing to more specialized tasks like 2D/3D modeling and financial forecasting and budgeting.

Reach out to us to know more about the tailored services we can offer to your company.

BUSINESS TRANSFORMATION

Our structured mechanisms for routine work help in streamlining the operations of the businesses and consequently lead to their positive transformation.

 

 

DATA SECURITY & CONFIDENTIALITY

Our multi level security protocols safeguard against any potential threat of leakage. The systems are encrypted and flow of data outside client network is restricted. Our employees undergo rigorous cyber security training.

 

 

SOP, & 24/7 AVAILABILITY

We offer 24/7 availability with three different shifts supporting you round the clock. This puts you way ahead of the competition and overall speed of the project is increased. Our engagements are governed by Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) document which is formulated collectively with the client during the onboarding phase.

PEER REVIEW & EMPLOYEE BACKUPS

Every employee has a backup within the same team. We make sure that there remains at least two subject matter experts for a single activity as we believe this leads to an ecosystem where people can review each other’s work productively, and act as backup in case the other person isn’t available.
